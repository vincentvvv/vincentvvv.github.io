Projects

HTML5 Slots Game
The first project I was assigned to work on was a HTML5 slots game. I was in charge of patching broken features in the game, specifically the backend call to Betable to get user data, the free spin feature, adding in the animations for winning reels, and also the payout lines to display the winnings.
SolveBrain
This game was requested by a client, and my main role at the beginning was to simply create a prototype. As I have never programmed in Flash before, I had to rewrite and do a lot of research before I finally got a working prototype. I have impressed the CTO with the prototype, and so he trusted me to continue and finally finish the game. I have a huge sense of ownership over this project as I was able to bring an idea from concept to completion. Moreover, I have worked closely with the lead artist to make decisions on the UI/UX of the game. In addition, I have implemented player analytics through the use of Flurry Analytics, which allowed me to learn about the analytics behind mobile games, specifically DAU, churn rates, funnels, and etc.
