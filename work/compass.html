Compass

Co-Op 3 | Software Engineer

Compass is a company that focuses on data driven real estate. I was a part of the Mobile team, that works on the Compass application.
Before coming to Compass, I had no real experience with building mobile applications. On my first day, it took me a while to get onboarded and familiarize myself with Xcode and also Android Studio. Luckily, I joined the team when the Android application was still in early development. Instead of using Java, we used Kotlin. As a result, I was able to spend most of my internship building the Android version of the application from the ground up. I have learned a lot about Android development, specifically the life cyle of Acticitivies, and Fragments, how intents work, and many more. In addition, I was also able to work on the iOS application. I was able to build many new features, specifically a mortgage calculator, more listing details, public collections, a routing class for deep linking, and etc. Both applications use Snail, a opensource Rx framework for Swift and Kotlin built by Compass.
I really liked how the Mobile team assigned tasks. Rather than having tasks assigned, I was able to choose whatever tasks that I wanted to work on. This has allowed me to take up many challenges, and it has also allowed me to work on tasks that interested me.
Aside from work, the culture at Compass was awesome! We had catered lunch everyday, delicious snacks, and lots of drinks in the fridge. The office was also dog friendly! Most importantly, at Compass, we love Bitmojis! They add a great sense of humour to make work fun and enjoyable.
