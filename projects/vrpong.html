VRPong

Virtual Reality

I have finally decided to invest in a Google Cardboard to dive into Virtual Reality! At the time, I was very addicted to ping pong and I wanted to bring it to the virtual world. With little experience in VR, I started out by playing around with the base SDK provided by google. From there, I realized that this would be very boring and so I decided to add a multiplayer component to this. This is accomplished through node.js and socket.io to achieve real-time gameplay.
