Global Game Jam

Worked together with two other people to create a platformer for the Global Game Jam. The game focused on a mechanic where the player is able to bounce off of the trail that the player leaves behind. This was developed with Unity3D and adobe photoshop for most of the art work. In the end, we won the game with the "Best Storyline"!
