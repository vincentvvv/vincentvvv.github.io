Broadway Lottery

Android Development

Download now on the Google Play Store!

During my internship at Compass in New York. I really wanted to watch Broadway shows. However, I did not want to pay full price for them. Luckily, there was the Broadway Lottery, where one can enter a lottery to obtain discounted tickets. I found it really annoying to always open my laptop and enter the form manually. Hence, I created Broadway Lottery to make it much easier to enter the lottery. The app scrapes the website to get the show times and when the lottery opens. The user can create profiles to be used to auto-fill the forms. If a lottery is not open yet, the user can add it to their calendar to be reminded when to enter the lottery. This app is built using Snail, a Rx Kotlin framework built by Compass. Moreover, the app currently has over 300+ downloads, and also over 800+ lottery entries!
I was very delighted when a co-worker from Compass, who uses Broadway Lottery daily, told me that he won 2 tickets to The Lion King!
